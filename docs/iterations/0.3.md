# Iteration 0.3.x Plan (Expansion)

## Direction

1. Revisit early stack and add missing high-value fun modules.
2. Introduce first visible `skyrim-llm-runtime` integration points.
3. Keep runtime integration additive and optional, not core-stability critical.
4. Continue UX-first expansion with controlled scope.
5. Preserve Patch Pack permissions and provenance rules.

## Release target

- Close iteration with Wabbajack release `0.4.0`.

## Candidate pillars

### World and cities overhaul (research snapshot, 2026-07-22)

Candidate: **JK's Skyrim** (https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/6289)

- v1.7, last updated 2024-01-28, 83.8k endorsements.
- All-in-one cities/towns/villages exterior overhaul. Large conflict surface (edits most exterior city/town cells, heavy clutter/lighting/navmesh).
- Fits the Patch Pack model: users install the mod and its third-party patches; VistulaRim ships only original compatibility work in `VistulaRim_Patch.esp`.

Scope rule for this pillar:

- Treat as a single major content pillar for the iteration (per the "one major pillar per iteration" rule in `docs/decisions.md`).
- Introduce in a small batch, not bundled with other world/visual pillars.
- Do not introduce during a stabilization iteration.

Required third-party patches against the current baseline:

| Patch | Baseline target | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| JK's Skyrim - Realistic Water Two Patch (v1.8+) | `Realistic Water Two SE` | Align RWT animated water troughs at Solitude/Markarth blacksmiths; add RWT water to 18 JK-added troughs |
| JK's Skyrim - Fishing (CC) Patch | `ccbgssse001-fish` (CC Fishing) | Fix clipping of Fishing-added objects/markers across towns/villages |
| JK's Skyrim - Gray Cowl (CC) Patch | `ccbgssse020-graycowl` (CC Gray Cowl) | Fix minor clipping in Riften and Solitude |
| JK's Skyrim - Arthmoor's Patch Pack (v1.4c) | Arthmoor village mods if added | Compatibility for Bring Out Your Dead, Cutting Room Floor, Dawnstar, Dragon Bridge, Falkreath, Ivarstead, Oblivion Gates, Rorikstead, Storefront |
| JK's Skyrim No Lights Patch (v1.2.0, optional) | Exterior lighting mods | Disable JK-added lights (lanterns/candles/torches/braziers) to reduce light-limit conflicts |

VistulaRim patch authoring required:

- Lighting reconciliation against Luminosity Lighting Overhaul and Relighting Skyrim (assess whether the No Lights optional patch or explicit forwardings into `VistulaRim_Patch.esp` is the correct path).
- Navmesh and exterior-clutter forwardings as justified by playtest symptoms.
- eFPS / occlusion review: JK's Skyrim is a known city framerate cost; validate `Skyrim Project Optimization SE` and `eFPS - Exterior FPS boost` interactions before sign-off.

Validation gates before this pillar is considered complete:

- Performance: city framerate smoke check (Whiterun, Solitude, Markarth, Windhelm, Riften).
- Lighting: no light-limit warnings in xEdit or in-game flicker in dense JK cells.
- Cross-platform: Ubuntu/Proton playtest required (stability KPI still holds).
- LOOT is sanity-only; do not treat JK-related dirty-plugin messages as automatic cleaning obligations.

Open questions (defer to milestone planning):

- Whether to use JK's Skyrim alone or evaluate against alternatives (e.g., `T.K.K.'s Total City`, `Dawn of Skyrim`, `C.W.A.`) before freezing the pillar.
- Whether any Arthmoor village mods in the Arthmoor Patch Pack should be added to the baseline first, or left to player choice.
